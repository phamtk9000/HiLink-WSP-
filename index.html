Premium Coworking & Serviced Offices in Central Hanoi

HiLink Workspaces operates seven characterful, design-led workspaces across the historic centre of Hanoi — in Hoàn Kiếm and Đống Đa, minutes from the Opera House, Hoàn Kiếm Lake and the central business district. From a single flexible desk to a fully fitted private floor, every location combines hospitality-grade service with enterprise-grade infrastructure: high-speed internet, meeting rooms, phone booths, printing, tea and coffee, and an on-site community team.

Workspace solutions

Private offices

Lockable, fully furnished serviced offices for teams of 2 to 100+, available on flexible monthly terms. Move-in ready, with utilities, cleaning, reception and meeting-room credits included in one transparent price.

Coworking & dedicated desks

Open coworking memberships and dedicated desks in light-filled shared lounges, with 24/7 access on selected plans and the freedom to work from any HiLink location in Hanoi.

Meeting rooms & event spaces

Boardrooms, training rooms and event venues bookable by the hour or by the day, with presentation screens, video-conferencing and catering options for teams, workshops and client sessions.

Virtual offices

A prestigious registered business address in central Hanoi with mail handling and company-registration support — the fastest way to establish a professional presence in Vietnam.

Locations across central Hanoi

Our workspaces are located at prime addresses including 60 Lý Thái Tổ in Hoàn Kiếm — steps from the Opera House and the State Bank — and 15 Tôn Thất Tùng in Đống Đa, close to Hanoi's university and medical district.
